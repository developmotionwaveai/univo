## What to See

### Dashboard (First Screen After Login)
- Welcome message
- 3 stat cards (My Clubs, Upcoming Events, Browse Clubs)
- Your clubs displayed in beautiful cards
- Suggested clubs to discover
- Links to explore more

### Navigation Menu (Left Sidebar)
- Dashboard
- Discover (see all 6 clubs)
- My Clubs (see 5 clubs you're in)
- My Events
- My Payments
- Notifications

### Test Club #1 (Special Admin View)
Click "Test Club #1" in "Your Clubs" section to see:
- Club detail page
- "Manage Club" or "Dashboard" button (admin access)
- Members list
- Events
- Applications

### Admin/Officer View
Once in club management:
- **Members Tab**: See all members, their roles, when they joined
- **Applications Tab**: Review pending applications, approve/reject
- **Events Tab**: Create/manage events
- **Dues Tab**: Set up club fees
- **Settings Tab**: Edit club info
